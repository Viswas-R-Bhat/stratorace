The Problem

Races are won and lost
in the pit lane

A single mistimed pit stop can cost a driver multiple grid positions. Human intuition is no longer sufficient against the speed of modern F1 racing.

How It Works

From raw lap data
to optimal strategy

StratoRace pipelines three seasons of Formula 1 telemetry through a feature engineering layer and into a trained PPO reinforcement learning agent that outputs live pit decisions.

📡 1
›
FastF1 Data
~80k laps across 2022–2024. Lap times, tyre compounds, sector splits, weather, positions.
⚙️ 2
›
Feature Engineering
LapTimeDelta, tyre degradation curves, stint analysis, gap deltas — domain-driven features built for strategy.
🧠 3
›
PPO Agent
Proximal Policy Optimisation trained on historical strategy outcomes, reward-shaped for position gain.
🏁 4
Pit Decision
Real-time compound recommendation and optimal pit lap with confidence score and SHAP explanation.
